Research Interests

  • Non-Smooth Analysis and non-smooth Lyapunov functions;

Short Bio

I am born in Udine, Italy. I received B.Sc and M.Sc degrees in Mathematics at the University of Udine, Italy, in 2015 and 2017, respectively. I then moved to Toulouse, France, to pursue my Ph.D. degree in Automatic Control at LAAS-CNRS laboratory, obtained in 2020. During my Ph.D I had the opportunity to visit the Control and Power Group at Imperial College, London between January-March 2020. I am currently postdoctoral researcher at the Université Catholique de Louvain (Louvain-La-Neuve, Belgium).
