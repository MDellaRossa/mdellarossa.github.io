Matteo Della Rossa

 

Assistant Professor (RTD-B, fixed-term, tenure-track) within the Automatica Research Group in DET (Dipartimento di Elettronica e Telecomunicazioni), Politecnico di Torino (Turin, Italy).

Research Interests

  • Hybrid (Switched) Dynamical Systems: Stability and Stabilization;
  • Non-Smooth Analysis and non-smooth Lyapunov functions;
  • Control applications of graphs and automata theory.
  • Optimal Control for Hybrid/Delay Systems

(Very) Short Bio

I received B.Sc and M.Sc degrees in Mathematics at the University of Udine, Italy, in 2015 and 2017, respectively. I then moved to Toulouse, France, to pursue my Ph.D. degree in Automatic Control at LAAS-CNRS laboratory, obtained in 2020. Between November 2020 and May 2023 I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Université Catholique de Louvain (Louvain-La-Neuve, Belgium). Between May 2025 and June 2025 I was a postdoctoral researcher (Assegnista di Ricerca) at the Università di Udine (Udine, Italy)
